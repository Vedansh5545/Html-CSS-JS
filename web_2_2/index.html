About Me

I’m a Computer Science junior at UNT with a passion for AI/ML, web development, and cybersecurity. By day, I build real-time ASL hand-recognition models in PyTorch (98% live accuracy) and design full-stack Angular/Firebase platforms used by 50+ ASL learners. By night, I prototype Chrome extensions, sketch robot concepts, and puzzle over AI ethics.

  • Developed a 3D pose-estimation pipeline that ranked in the top 10% on the MotionAGFormer leaderboard.
  • Launched an ASL learning web app that’s helped learners master 10+ letters with interactive demos.

Outside of code, I’m a die-hard Valorant player and an avid reader of sci-fi.
